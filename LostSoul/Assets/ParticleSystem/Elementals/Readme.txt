Elementals 1.1.6
------------------------------------------------------------------

	This package contains 53 fantastic Shuriken particles that are categorized to 8 kinds of element.

	Features:
	
		• Fire (28)
		• Water (5)
		• Wind (2)
		• Earth (3)
		• Thunder (5)
		• Ice (4)
		• Light (4)
		• Darkness (2)

		• Support all build player platforms.
		
	Compatible:

		• Unity 4.7.1 or higher.
		• Unity 5.3.4 or higher.

Using demo scene
------------------------------------------------------------------

	1. Open Demo in "Elementals/Demo/Scenes/Elementals Demo (960x600px)".
	2. Press Play button.
	3. Camera control
		Orbit:		mouse drag (single touch move on touch screen device).
		Zoom:		Mouse wheel (two fingers pinch/scroll on touch screen device).
	4. Particle test.
		Category:	Up/Down keys or click/tap on previous/next buttons.
		Particles:	Left/Right keys or click/tap on previous/next buttons.
